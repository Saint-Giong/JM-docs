## Meeting Guidelines

### Documentation Standards

All meeting minutes must include:

- Date, time, and location
- Meeting facilitator and note taker
- List of attendees
- Agenda items with presenters
- Action items with assignees and deadlines
- Special notes and conclusions

### Meeting Types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Daily Standup">
    **Time:** 10:00 AM (weekdays)
    **Duration:** 15 minutes
    **Platform:** Discord
    **Purpose:** Quick progress updates and blocker identification
  </Tab>
