## Welcome to SGJM Documentation

**SGJM (Saint Giong Job Manager)** is Team Saint Giong's implementation of the Job Manager Subsystem for DEVision, a comprehensive job matching platform that bridges job applicants in Computer Science fields with their potential employers.

## Our Mission

DEVision embodies the Vietnamese proverb "An Cư Lạc Nghiệp" (Settled and Flourished), emphasizing the importance of establishing a solid and fulfilling career as the foundation for prosperity and personal happiness.
